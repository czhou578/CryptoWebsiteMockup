About Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the famed cryptocurrency developed by Satoshi Nakomoto in 2009. It has now hit 1 million per coin and has even persuaded Peter Schiff himself to jump on. There are now mining centers all over the world and are threatening the hegemony of the US dollar.

About Ethereum

Ethereum was created by Vitalik Buterin in 2015 and focuses on smart contract functionality, allowing users to bypass third party negotiators and institutions for acts such as buying a house. It has now reached $10,000 in value

About Altcoins

Dogecoin was created by a group of memers who wanted a fun to play with crypto. It was pumped to 100$ per coin by Elon Musk in 2025. It is now serving as the prime currency on the Mars 1 lunar base. The phrase "Doge to the moon" really happened.
